PERFECT LOVE INTERNATIONAL

ABOUT US

Perfect Love International (PLI) was established in July, 2009 with a mission of; ‘equipping and empowering individuals; especially the youth through the development, hosting and promotion of programs that encourage and foster Love’. The vision of PLI is to; ‘equip and develop young people for life, love and leadership. Perfect Love International was born out of a desire to make our communities better through love. To achieve this objective, young people from age ten (10) years and above are targeted for the transformational events and programmes. PLI is highly committed to its course. Thus, several events and outreach programs have been successfully organized and well attended by the target group so far. These activities are ‘Youth Rally’, ‘A love Story’, ‘Transformation’ and ‘Cutting Edge Leadership’. An elaboration on the framework and various activities have been attached as ‘appendices’.
